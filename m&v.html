Mission and vision

Our Mission and Vision

Our misson

How are you supposed to grow your book of business while doing the job of 10 people?

Our mission is to provide you with all the support you need in order to free up your valuable time to pursue additional customers and shipments. Getting the job done at a suitable price, means having the best equipment in the right location. In order to accomplish this, you need a company that has the size and ability to deliver when and where you need it. Taylor Shipping Solutions, as an independent agent for Landstar Agency AUS, can ensure precisely that. Spend your time ensuring your customers are satisfied and less on paperwork and monitoring drivers.

Our vision

Our vision is to grow Taylor Shipping Solutions to the point where all of your transportation needs are covered without a hitch!

We don’t want you to worry about having to switch between companies that are able to provide you with the solutions you need. Our team of highly skilled and strongly motivated Freight Agents is here to support you by providing the equipment and certifications you need to get any shipment managed properly. Commitment to the work we do alongside our connections and relationships with our drivers have proven to be the key to our success story and we will continue to grow and improve. No matter what you need, we have the perfect solution.
