Landstar

Taylor Shipping Solutions is a transportation company operating as an independent Landstar agent for brokering. We provide transportation services from over the road to air freight. We are dedicated to each and every customer and partner we have and we believe that there is strength in numbers so with the combined forces of Taylor Shipping Solutions and Landstar, you will always get the best possible solution.

Taylor Shipping Solutions is one of the top 5 agencies working closely with Landstar. By utilizing Landstar's equipment and having a team of highly educated freight agents at your disposal anytime, we can coordinate and move your shipment at any time and to any location in the US, which is our main advantage that no other company can reach. The relationships we have with our partners allow us to improve each other by giving constant feedback regarding the rate, service and anything and everything else involved in the transportation of your goods.
